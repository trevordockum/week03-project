Technology Behind Our Modules

All of the modules we use for installation are "Tier 1" solar modules. Tier 1 solar panels are rated as the highest performing solar panels on the market. They are evaluted by the following metrics.

Tier Class Durability Efficiency Performance Warranty
1 Premium 19% - 23% 25 - 30 years
2 Mid-Grade 15% - 18% 20 - 25 years
3 Low-Grade <15% 10 - 15 years



All modules that we offer are 100% Tier 1 panels that are constructed entirely within the United States. Our company's belief, and vision is to offer the best products, at a competitive price. Solar panel manufactures internal to the United States receive additional tax incentives and goverment funding, and we pass these savings down to EVERY "Astro Powered" home.


All of our products come with a 25-year produciton warranty, and 25-year service warranty as well. Not only will your system be guaranteed to hit a minimum production requirement yearly, but if we do not hit (or exceed) the guaranteed production for your system you will be compensated for the loss in energy! We hold ourselves to this standard so there is more savings, more protection, and more power in your hands.
